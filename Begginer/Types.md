## Common Types in C#

- **`int`**: Whole numbers (e.g., `1`, `-56`, `948`).  
- **`double`**: Decimal numbers (e.g., `239.43909`, `-660.01`).  
- **`char`**: Single characters (e.g., `'a'`, `'&'`, `'Â£'`).  
- **`string`**: Sequence of characters (e.g., `"dog"`, `"hello world"`).  
- **`bool`**: Boolean values (`true` or `false`).
